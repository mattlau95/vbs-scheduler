## Executive summary

The app is functionally solid and visually clean, but has a cluster of accessibility violations that are mostly one-line CSS fixes. The biggest single issue is color contrast: the brand green (`#2f8a5f`) fails WCAG AA at 4.27:1 (threshold: 4.5:1) and it's used everywhere вЂ” active chips, links, the summary count. The practice badge is worse at 3.24:1. Beyond contrast, the tab bar and custom toggles have no ARIA so screen readers can't interpret them. LCP is 2.9s against a 2.5s target, caused by a render-blocking Google Fonts call with no preconnect.

**4 blockers, 6 important, 2 polish.**

**Snapshot**

- Checklist items passing: **22 / 32**
- Core Web Vitals: LCP **2.9s** В· INP **n/a (lab)** В· CLS **0** *(2026 thresholds: <2.5s / <200ms / <0.1)*
- Accessibility (axe): **24 violations** вЂ” color-contrast (16), landmark-one-main (1), region (7)
- pa11y: **17 errors** вЂ” all contrast (4.27:1 and 3.24:1)
